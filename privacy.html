Privacy Policy for Cucina

Last updated: December 17, 2024

Introduction

Thank you for using Cucina ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Cucina is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Recipes You Save: Recipe titles, images, URLs, and content you save are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Recipe Ingredients: When you use the AI-powered ingredient extraction feature, recipe text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing.
  • API Key (Optional): If you provide your own Anthropic API key, it is stored securely in your device's keychain.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Cucina does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for app functionality:

  • Recipes: Stored locally on your device to display your recipe collection
  • Ingredient Extraction: Recipe text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to identify and parse ingredients
  • Reminders Integration: With your permission, ingredients are added to your Apple Reminders app

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All recipes and folders are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Secure Storage: API keys are stored in iOS Keychain, Apple's secure credential storage system
  • No Cloud Sync: Currently, data is not automatically synced to cloud services (iCloud sync may be added in future updates)

Third-Party Services

Cucina uses the following third-party services:

Anthropic Claude API

  • Purpose: AI-powered ingredient extraction from recipes
  • Data Shared: Recipe text content only
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

Apple Reminders

  • Purpose: Adding ingredients to your grocery lists
  • Data Shared: Ingredient names (only with your explicit permission)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can delete individual recipes or all recipes at any time within the app
  • Reminders Access: You can revoke Reminders access in iOS Settings → Privacy → Reminders
  • API Key: You can remove or change your API key at any time in Settings
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device

Children's Privacy

Cucina is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

Your recipes and data are stored on your device until you choose to delete them or uninstall the app. We do not store your data on our servers.

International Data Transfers

When using the AI ingredient extraction feature, recipe text may be processed by Anthropic's servers, which may be located outside your country. Anthropic is committed to data protection and complies with applicable data protection laws.
